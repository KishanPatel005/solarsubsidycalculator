## FAQ

### What is the price of Tata Solar 3kW system in India 2026?

A common planning benchmark is **~₹1,90,000 before subsidy**. After the central subsidy cap (up to **₹78,000**), the out-of-pocket cost can be around **~₹1,12,000**, depending on the final approved capacity and system design.

### Is Tata Solar eligible for PM Surya Ghar subsidy?

Subsidy eligibility is tied to the **PM Surya Ghar** process: residential consumer eligibility, DISCOM feasibility, installation via approved vendor pathway, and commissioning/verification on the portal. The module brand alone does not guarantee eligibility.

### Which is better — Tata Solar or Waaree?

Both are widely used. Compare like-for-like module series, warranty terms, service responsiveness in your city, and the installer quality. If service reliability is your top priority, choose the brand + installer combination with the best local support track record.

### What warranty does Tata Solar offer in India?

Many Tata module lines advertise long warranties (often 25 years for performance; some lines higher). Always confirm **product vs performance warranty**, inverter warranty, and who provides service (dealer vs OEM).

### How to find Tata Solar dealer near me?

Start with Tata Power Solar’s official website dealer/partner listings, then shortlist 2–3 authorized dealers and compare quotations with the same system specifications.
